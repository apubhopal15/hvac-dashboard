📋 What is this?

This HVAC Dashboard is designed to calculate and analyze duct velocity for HVAC systems at ESIC Hospital Pithampur. Upload your Excel file with HVAC equipment schedules, and the dashboard will automatically calculate all velocity parameters and validate them against CPWD (Central Public Works Department) standards.

📁 Excel Integration

Upload your HVAC data files in Excel format (.xlsx, .xls) and get instant calculations.

📊 Real-time Analysis

Automatic velocity calculations with instant status validation against CPWD limits.

💾 Export Results

Download your calculations as CSV files for reports and documentation.

📱 Responsive Design

Access the dashboard from desktop, tablet, or mobile devices.

🎯 Status Indicators

Clear visual indicators show which units meet CPWD standards and which need revision.

📐 Automatic Calculations

All HVAC velocity formulas calculated automatically with high precision.

🚀 Getting Started

Option 1 - Quick Start: Click "Open Dashboard" to access the calculator immediately.

Option 2 - Load Sample Data: Once in the dashboard, click "Load Sample Data" to see example calculations.

Option 3 - Upload Your Data: Click "Upload Excel File" and select your HVAC equipment schedule file.

For detailed instructions, see the Quick Start Guide.
